A true HR strategy is proactive and delivers the tools, environment, structure and resources to members need to achieve organisational goals. A strong HR Does:
• Identify and hire the right people
• Manage and grow those people in the right way
• Develop each role to achieve your business strategy
• Build a company culture that supports your customer promise

Improving overall experience of members.

#Put your people first: The first and probably most important step to facilitating member experience that will increase our team’s loyalty to the cell is creating a human-centered workplace. Making it a priority to earn our members’ trust in organisational leadership through transparency, honesty and inspiration.
#Reveal purpose: Members don’t want to just show and up and perform a task without knowing why they’re there in the first place. They want to know how the dots connect, and how their contributions fit into the bigger picture.
#Act on member feedback: Many organizations fail to take this crucial step. As a result, the time and effort spent soliciting feedback is essentially wasted, and the member experience suffers.
